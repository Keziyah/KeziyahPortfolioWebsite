The Adventure

The Bladress is undertaking a one-woman rollerblading expedition across Europe, covering 11,000 km, to empower women and young girls. While raising money for girls’ education, she will attempt to set the Guinness World Record for the longest journey on inline skates.

The Bladress is relying on the kindness of strangers to provide housing, food, and other necesities throughout this trip. If you'd like to participate when she comes to your city, sign up and let us know how you can help.

About The Bladress

bladress /ˈbleɪd·rɛs/ n. - a female rollerblader

Yanise was born and raised in Hong Kong, went to high school in Italy at age 16, attended college in the US, worked in the Dominican Republic, and founded The Bladress, LLC. in New York. She is constantly on the move for new adventures!

The Mission

1. Represent Women's Empowerment

The Bladress will use her strength and endurance to fight for womens’ equality.

2. Resurrect the lost art of Trust

She will harness the good of strangers to power her through every journey.

3. Give and take

From the love and kindness shown on her journeys, The Bladress will give back to girls in need around the globe.
